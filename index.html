Solar Ambience

Welcome to Solar Ambience. The aim of this website is to help you create your very own ambience.

Maybe you need to concentrate, meditate, study, sleep or just leave some background noise on for your dog.

Use the YouTube videos below to do this. You can play as many or as little videos as you like and use the YouTube volume control to adjust the volume of your ambience. Have Fun!



Solar Ambience is set in the key of A Sharp/B Flat.
